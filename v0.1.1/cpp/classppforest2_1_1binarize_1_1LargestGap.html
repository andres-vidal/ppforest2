Detailed Description

Binarization by largest gap between sorted projected group means.

Projects group means onto 1D via the projector, sorts by projected mean, and finds the largest gap between consecutive means. Groups below the gap become binary group 0, groups above become binary group 1. This is the default PPtree binarization method.
